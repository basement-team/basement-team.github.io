The Basement Team's new website; daily news & blogs from devs

Basement team recently announced their new website, it will be bringing dev news and daily cool blogs from the developers, developers who would like to take part of the blogs are welcome to join the team.

The website is hosted in github pages, and the source code is available on github. Currently maintained by the staff from the basement discord community.

The website is currently under construction, and will be updated regularly.
